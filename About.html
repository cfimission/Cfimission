Man of God

Engaging God’s Word Through Faith

Being burdened by God, Dr.Asher Andrew founded The Life Temple near Hitech City, Hyderabad. The Life Temple is church based on the vision of Acts 2:42 (Apostles’ teaching, fellowship of the saints, breaking of bread-Lord’s table and prayers). Every Sunday, powerful life changing messages are delivered by Dr. Asher Andrew in all 3 services. English Service timings 7:30 AM-9:00 AM, the first Telugu service timings 9:00 AM-11:00 AM, and the second Telugu service timings 11:30 AM-1:30 PM every Sunday. It’s the right place for those desiring to worship the Lord in spirit and truth, for experiencing abundance of life and for a divine life changing encounter with Christ. Dr Asher Andrew is an anointed revival international speaker and a man of God who has preached across the globe in several countries and impacted many through his penetrating messages and songs. He is Founder of The Life Temple, which is the fastest growing church in Hitech city, Hyderabad. He is a writer of more than 50 heart touching songs such as Antha Na meluke, Ooruko na pranama and many others having millions of viewers. He is a double gold medalist i.e both in post Graduation and Ph.D in Health Psychology from the Central University of Hyderabad. He represented India at the World Congress Canada 2018. He is a founder chairman of Abundant Life Foundation where thousands have been relieved of their psychological pain. He is also the chairman of The Life Temple Medical Services which stood with the people even in the utmost prevailing times of Covid.
